About Us

The platform e-challenge.me is the first website of this kind on the whole Internet where you can both create and take up challenges, contributing to building an international, twicky community of people who simply have the will and courage to share their abilities and passions with the world!

Our mission is to persuade people to get out their comfort zone, as we know that this is the first step towards the personal growth and development, as well as the first step towards making the world a place filled with people who use their potential to the full! So, dear friends, pluck up the courage to showcase your wonderful, exceptional skills and also encourage others to challenge themselves!

We already have plenty of ideas for further improvements, like implementing chat, possibility to redeem points collected while participating in challenges for products of well-known brands and many others which we won’t reveal now to make you amazed once they have been brought on.We will be very grateful for your contribution in creating this portal, so all these amazing surprises can be introduced. The truth is that everyone is able to cross their limits and do the things they would probably never expect from themselves if one day they wouldn’t have stumbled upon a person who simply had thrown them a challenge…
